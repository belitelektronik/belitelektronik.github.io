Box builds

Belit Electronics excels at all types of box and device build applications. Our in-house cable assembly capabilities enhance our programmatic and technical experience for these types of applications.

Advantages:

  • Single point of purchase
  • Design assistance and engineering support
  • Documentation

Belit Electronics uses various testing equipment to 100% quality test all of our box build applications.
