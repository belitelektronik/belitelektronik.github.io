Military cable assemblies

Relying on its high quality standards, knowledge and experience Belit Electronics has been meeting the military cable assembly demands of the defence industry for over 15 years.

Based on the requirements of the industry, for high quality workmanship, the personnel has been trained according to IPC-A-620.

All cables, connectors, terminals and cable lugs are conducted using approved material, correct equipment and based on the correct production techniques.

Through the use of various test equipements cable assemblies can be tested 100% for quality.

Therefore problems that can be experienced in the future can be forseen and prevented. High attention is given to testing short circuits, open, false and discontinous cable assemblies are determined and not used.
