Soldering

For many cable assemblies connections soldering is being conducted manually.

The trustworthyness of this connection is based on the workmanship quality.

The only way workmanship quality can be provied is through personnel training.

The personnel who is conducting the soldering and those conducting the testing are trained in regards to the IPC-A-610D standard. For preparation before assembly cable tips can be soldered in a pot, as a result we can stop the tips of the cales from dispersing. In the soldering of cable tips the solder should be dispersed on the wires, the wires in the solder point should be visible to the eye. The appropriate equipment, and the approved solder wire use influeneces the solder connection quality and reliability.

Belit Electronics only uses approved solder wires, solder and flux.
