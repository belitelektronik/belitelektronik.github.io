Flat cable assembly and IDC

Belit Electronics offers high quality and reliable flat cable assembly and IDC connector assembly services.

IDC (insulation displacement connection) is a method for terminating an insulated wire to a connector or terminal without pre-stripping the insulation from the connector.

The IDC method allows the flat cables and single core wires to be connected in a fast and low cost manner.

Belit Electronics has years of experience in working with flat cable assemblies.

Using the correct tooling ensures a repeatable process and thereby reduces potential assembly errors. At the same time the flat cable and the IDC connector quality is also very important in this type of connection and especially the wires coating hardness and material connection.

All flat cables with IDC connectors are 100% electronically tested.
